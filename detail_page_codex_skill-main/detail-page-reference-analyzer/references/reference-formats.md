# Reference Detail Page Formats

## 유료 고가 강의형

Purpose: sell a paid course, cohort, consulting package, or coaching product directly.

Typical sequence:

1. Big result promise and instructor authority.
2. Real schedule and curriculum.
3. Challenge, feedback, assignment, or execution support.
4. Discount, deadline, and price justification.
5. Benefits stack.
6. Market desire or problem logic.
7. New leverage such as AI, automation, sourcing, or templates.
8. Risk removal.
9. Proof: student results, screenshots, photos, lectures.
10. Instructor story and final CTA.

Recommended production shape: 10-12 separate mobile cuts.

## 무료강의 신청형

Purpose: capture leads for a live webinar, free class, Kakao room, mailing list, or later paid offer.

Typical sequence:

1. Free live date/time and low-friction CTA.
2. Free bonus or immediate reward.
3. Big market opportunity and money smell.
4. Target audience callout.
5. Demand proof, search proof, or conversion proof.
6. Beginner risk removal.
7. Instructor credibility and field photos.
8. Core bottleneck that the instructor solves.
9. Repeat date/time and free application CTA.

Recommended production shape: 2-4 long mobile images or 6-8 shorter sections.

## 전자책/자료 다운로드형

Purpose: exchange a practical guide, checklist, template, or PDF for contact information.

Typical sequence:

1. Concrete file promise.
2. Who needs it now.
3. Preview of pages/templates/checklists.
4. Pain avoided or time saved.
5. Simple CTA to receive the resource.

Recommended production shape: 1-3 images.

## 상품 판매형

Purpose: sell a physical product through a marketplace or brand store.

Typical sequence:

1. Product hero and main benefit.
2. Problem or use case.
3. Differentiator.
4. Detail/texture/components.
5. Usage scene.
6. Options/specs/size.
7. Proof/review if provided.
8. Shipping/package/caution.

Recommended production shape: 8-12 mobile cuts.
