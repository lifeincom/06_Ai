# Signal Pet

## Concept

Signal is a compact Codex-style operator pet based on the user's working pattern: practical, fast-moving, evidence-focused, repo-aware, and built around shipping useful artifacts instead of decoration.

## Visual Direction

- Small pixel-art-adjacent digital pet with chibi proportions.
- Chunky readable silhouette with a thick dark outline.
- Calm focused face, tiny limbs, and a stance that feels ready to work.
- Palette: charcoal outline, warm off-white body, muted green accent, small amber status mark.
- Avoid: purple-dominant palette, gradients, scenery, UI panels, text, fake symbols, and complex tiny props.

## Personality

- Quietly decisive.
- Prefers exact paths, concrete outputs, and verified claims.
- Treats "ship it" as the default state.
- Looks calm during large diffs and mildly unimpressed by vague plans.

## Base Generation Prompt

Create a single clean reference sprite for a Codex app digital pet named Signal.

Signal is a compact Codex-style digital pet based on a pragmatic operator: fast-moving, evidence-focused, repo-aware, and built for execution. It should feel like a calm companion for coding, dashboards, market checks, content systems, and shipping work.

Use a pixel-art-adjacent low-resolution mascot sprite style with compact chibi proportions, chunky whole-body silhouette, thick dark 1-2 px outline, visible stepped/pixel edges, limited palette, flat cel shading, simple readable focused face, and tiny limbs. Palette: charcoal outline, warm off-white body, muted green accent, and a small amber status mark. No purple-dominant palette.

Output one centered full-body pet sprite pose only, on a perfectly flat pure magenta #FF00FF chroma-key background. The pet must be fully visible and readable as a tiny digital pet suitable for animation into a 192x208 sprite cell. Do not include scenery, text, labels, borders, checkerboard transparency, detached effects, shadows, glows, or extra props.
